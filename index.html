About Us

Welcome to our research group EpioNe (Epidemics on Networks). Our focus is on rigorously understanding the behavior of epidemics and other spreading processes on networks. We borrow tools from network science, dynamical systems, optimization, game theory and signal processing for modeling, estimation and control of epidemics on networks.
